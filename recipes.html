🍗 Chicken Biryani

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 Large Onions (thinly sliced & fried)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Biryani Masala (as required)
  • Fresh Coriander & Mint Leaves
  • Saffron soaked in milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or Ghee (for cooking)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chili, turmeric, biryani masala, and salt. Let it rest for 1 hour.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for layering.
  3. Add tomatoes to remaining onions and cook until soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until chicken is tender and oil separates.
  5. In another pot, boil rice with salt until 80% cooked. Drain the water.
  6. Now layer chicken gravy and rice. Add fried onions, coriander, mint, saffron milk between layers.
  7. Cover with lid and steam cook (dum) on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.

Enjoy your delicious Chicken Biryani! 😋

🥔 Aloo Keema (Minced Meat with Potatoes)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Minced Meat (Beef or Chicken)
  • 2 medium Potatoes (peeled & cubed)
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and chopped onion. Fry until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add minced meat and cook until color changes and water dries.
  4. Add tomatoes, chili, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add potatoes and 1/2 cup water. Cover and cook until potatoes are soft.
  6. Add garam masala and simmer for 5 minutes uncovered.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Serve hot with roti or naan! 🫓

🥬 Palak Paneer (Spinach with Cottage Cheese)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 250g Paneer (cubed)
  • 3 cups Spinach (washed & chopped)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Cream (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Boil spinach in water for 2-3 minutes, then blend to make a smooth puree.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and fry chopped onions till golden.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add tomatoes, green chilies, salt, turmeric, and red chili. Cook till soft.
  5. Add spinach puree and mix well. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Optional: Add cream for extra richness.

Serve hot with naan or jeera rice! 😋

🍗 Chicken Karahi

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Chicken (with bone)
  • 3 Tomatoes (blended or chopped)
  • 2 Green Chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Crushed Coriander
  • 1 tsp Crushed Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup Oil or Ghee
  • Fresh Ginger (julienned for garnish)
  • Fresh Coriander (chopped)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a wok (karahi). Add chicken and fry for 5–6 minutes until lightly golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add blended tomatoes, salt, red chili, turmeric, coriander, and cumin. Cook for 15–20 minutes on medium flame.
  4. Cook until oil separates and chicken is tender.
  5. Add green chilies and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala, garnish with julienned ginger and fresh coriander.

Serve hot with naan or roti! 🔥

🍖 Nihari (Slow-Cooked Beef Stew)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Beef (shank or bong meat)
  • 1/2 cup Oil or Ghee
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 cup Wheat Flour (atta)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 cups Water (or as needed)
  • Fresh Ginger (julienned for garnish)
  • Fresh Coriander (chopped)
  • Lemon wedges and Green Chilies (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot and fry sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add beef and fry until it changes color.
  4. Add red chili, turmeric, coriander, and salt. Cook for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Add enough water to cover meat. Cover and cook on low flame for 2–3 hours until meat is tender.
  6. In a small bowl, mix wheat flour with water to make a smooth paste. Slowly add to the nihari to thicken.
  7. Simmer for 15 more minutes and adjust thickness as needed.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala, garnish with fried onions, julienned ginger, and fresh coriander.

🍲 Haleem (Spiced Lentil & Meat Stew)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Boneless Beef or Chicken
  • 1/2 cup Chana Dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1/4 cup Masoor Dal (red lentils)
  • 1/4 cup Moong Dal (yellow lentils)
  • 1/4 cup Broken Wheat (dalia)
  • 2 Onions (sliced and fried)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup Oil or Ghee
  • Fresh Coriander, Mint & Ginger (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Soak lentils and wheat for 2–3 hours. Boil them together until soft and mushy. Blend if needed.
  2. In a separate pot, heat oil and fry onions until golden. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste, then meat. Cook until it browns and water dries.
  4. Add salt, chili, turmeric, and cook with 2–3 cups of water until meat is very tender.
  5. Shred the meat and mix it with the blended lentils.
  6. Cook together on low flame, stirring often to avoid sticking.
  7. Add garam masala, adjust salt and spice, and cook until thick.
  8. Garnish with fried onions, coriander, mint, and ginger. Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Serve with naan or khameeri roti for a hearty meal! 😋

🍛 Chicken Korma

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Chicken (bone-in pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 large Onions (sliced and fried)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 cup Oil or Ghee
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 4 Green Cardamoms
  • 2 Black Cardamoms
  • 1 small piece of Cinnamon
  • 2–3 Cloves
  • Salt to taste
  • Kewra water (optional – a few drops)
  • Almonds or Cashew paste (optional – for richness)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pot. Add whole spices (cardamoms, cinnamon, cloves) and let them sizzle.
  2. Add chicken and sauté until it changes color.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Mix in yogurt, red chili, turmeric, coriander, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add half of the fried onions and cook on medium heat until oil separates.
  6. Add a little water (if needed), cover, and cook till chicken is tender.
  7. Add garam masala, kewra water, and remaining fried onions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Optional: Add almond or cashew paste for a royal flavor.

Serve hot with naan or sheermal! ✨

🥩 Seekh Kabab

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Minced Beef or Chicken (finely ground)
  • 1 Onion (grated and squeezed)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2–3 Green Chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Crushed Cumin
  • 1 tsp Crushed Coriander Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter (for brushing)
  • Skewers (metal or wooden)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine minced meat with onion, ginger garlic paste, green chilies, spices, and herbs.
  2. Mix very well and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Wet your hands and shape the mixture around skewers like long cylinders (seekh shape).
  4. Grill or cook on a hot pan with light oil, turning sides until fully cooked and browned.
  5. Alternatively, bake in oven at 200°C for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Brush with butter or ghee while cooking for extra flavor.

Serve hot with chutney, naan, and lemon wedges! 🍋

🍚 Daal Chawal (Lentils with Rice)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Masoor or Moong Dal (lentils)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups Water
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • 1 small Onion (sliced for tarka)
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Cooked White Rice (for serving)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak dal for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a pot, add dal, water, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Boil and cook until soft.
  3. Mash slightly for a smooth texture.
  4. In a small pan, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and sliced onion. Fry until golden brown.
  5. Pour this tarka (tempering) over the cooked dal and mix.
  6. Serve hot with freshly boiled white rice (chawal).

A comfort meal best enjoyed with pickles or papad! 🥄

🌿 Bhindi Masala (Okra Curry)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Bhindi (Okra/Ladyfinger)
  • 2 medium Onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2–3 Green Chilies (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp Oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry bhindi completely. Cut into 1-inch pieces.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp oil and lightly fry bhindi for 7–10 minutes until soft. Set aside.
  3. In a separate pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  4. Add tomatoes, green chilies, and all spices. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add fried bhindi to the masala and stir gently.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 5–7 minutes. Avoid overmixing to keep bhindi intact.

Serve with chapati or rice for a simple, tasty meal! 🍽

🥚 Anda Curry (Egg Curry)

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 4–6 Eggs (boiled and peeled)
  • 2 medium Onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup Oil
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Fry boiled eggs until light golden (optional). Set aside.
  2. In the same oil, add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste, stir well.
  4. Add tomatoes, salt, and all spices. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil separates.
  5. Add yogurt and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add 1 cup water for gravy and bring to a boil.
  7. Add fried eggs into the gravy and simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Delicious with paratha, naan, or boiled rice! 🍛

🥩 Shami Kabab

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Beef or Chicken Mince (keema)
  • 1/2 cup Chana Daal (soaked for 1 hour)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Egg (for binding)
  • Fresh Coriander & Mint (chopped)
  • Oil for frying

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. In a pot, add mince, soaked chana daal, onion, chilies, garlic ginger paste, spices, and enough water to cook.
  2. Cook until all water dries and everything is soft. Let it cool.
  3. Grind the mixture into a fine paste. Add chopped coriander, mint, and 1 egg for binding.
  4. Make round kabab shapes and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  5. Shallow fry on medium heat until golden brown on both sides.

Serve hot with ketchup or mint chutney! 🌿

🍗 White Chicken Handi

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (boneless or with bones)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt (whisked)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 medium Onion (finely chopped or paste)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2–3 Green Chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tsp White Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until soft (not browned).
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until color changes to white.
  4. Add yogurt, white pepper, black pepper, and salt. Cook until chicken is tender and oil separates.
  5. Add cream and green chilies. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander or extra cream (optional).

Serve with naan or paratha for a rich restaurant-style meal! 🍽️

🥘 Mutton Korma

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Mutton (with bones)
  • 2 medium Onions (finely sliced)
  • 1 cup Yogurt (whisked)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 4–5 Cardamoms
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 Cloves
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup Oil or Ghee
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Fried onions for garnish (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon) and sauté for a few seconds.
  2. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and fry for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Add mutton and cook until the meat changes color.
  5. Add red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add yogurt and cook on medium heat until oil separates.
  7. Add 1–1.5 cups of water. Cover and cook until mutton is tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fried onions.

Serve hot with naan or boiled rice. Enjoy this royal feast! 👑

🥦 Mix Vegetable

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 cup Carrots (sliced)
  • 1 cup Peas
  • 1 cup Cauliflower florets
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste, cook for a minute, then add tomatoes.
  4. Add all spices and cook until tomatoes soften.
  5. Add all vegetables, mix well, and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Add a splash of water, cover, and cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.

Best served with roti, paratha, or as a side dish! 🥙

🥗 Chana Chaat

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups Boiled Chickpeas (white chana)
  • 1 medium Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (chopped)
  • 1 Boiled Potato (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp Green Chutney
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Chutney
  • 1/2 tsp Chaat Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish
  • Sev or Papri for topping (optional)
  • Lemon juice to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix boiled chickpeas, potato, onion, tomato, and green chili.
  2. Add green chutney, tamarind chutney, lemon juice, and all spices.
  3. Mix everything well and let it sit for 5–10 minutes to absorb flavors.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander, sev or papri (optional).

Enjoy chilled or at room temperature. Perfect for iftar or tea time! 🕔

🍗 Chicken Tikka

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (boneless cubes or leg pieces)
  • 3 tbsp Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Orange or red food color (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix all ingredients to prepare marinade.
  2. Add chicken pieces, coat well, and let it marinate for at least 2 hours (or overnight for best results).
  3. Grill on skewers over charcoal, bake at 200°C for 20–25 minutes, or cook in a pan until done.
  4. Brush with butter or oil while grilling for extra flavor.
  5. Garnish with lemon wedges and onion rings.

Serve hot with mint chutney and naan! 🔥

🍛 Rajma Masala

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rajma (red kidney beans)
  • 2 medium Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (blended or finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Soak rajma overnight, then boil until soft (pressure cooker: 4–5 whistles).
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add all spices and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Add boiled rajma with water, simmer for 15–20 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Enjoy with steamed rice or roti. Super comforting! 🍚

🍮 Suji Halwa

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Suji (semolina)
  • 1/2 cup Ghee (clarified butter)
  • 3/4 cup Sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2.5 cups Water
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Almonds & Pistachios (for garnish)
  • Raisins (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and roast suji on medium flame till golden and aromatic.
  2. In another pan, boil water and sugar together to make sugar syrup.
  3. Slowly add the hot syrup to the roasted suji (carefully, it will splatter).
  4. Stir continuously to avoid lumps. Add cardamom powder.
  5. Cook until the ghee separates and halwa thickens.
  6. Garnish with nuts and serve warm.

Perfect for breakfast or festive desserts! ✨
